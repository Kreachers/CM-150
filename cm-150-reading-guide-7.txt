Reading Guide
THE CONVERSATIONS: WALTER MURCH AND THE ART OF EDITING FILM, P.112-125

1. What does Murch mean by metaphorical vs. realistic sound in 8ilm? Explain and describe an example from a film you’ve seen.



2. Murch mentions birdsong as a cliched sound to ‘lighten’ a 8ilm’s mood. Name and describe at least two other sound cliches used to control viewers’ emotions in 8ilm and television.



3. What does Murch mean when he describes his technique of “worldizing”? How might this be applicable to sound design in games?



4. What does Murch argue is the correct use of music in 8ilm? Do you agree or disagree? Explain.
